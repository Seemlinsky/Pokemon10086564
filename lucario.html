Lucario

Lucario is a Fighting/Steel-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV. It is a bipedal, canine-like Pokémon with a blue and black furred body. Lucario has a white mask-like face with red eyes and a yellow nose. It has long, pointed ears with black tips and a tuft of fur on its head. Lucario's arms and legs are covered in metal armor, and it has a long, metal tail.

Catching Rate

The catching rate of Lucario is determined by various factors, including the player's level, the type of Poké Ball used, and any status effects inflicted on the Pokémon. The base catching rate for wild Lucario is typically around 90%.

Shiny Rate

Shiny Pokémon, including Shiny Lucario, have a rare alternate coloration. The shiny rate for Lucario, like most Pokémon, is approximately 1 in 4,096 encounters. This means it can be quite challenging to find a shiny Lucario in the wild.
