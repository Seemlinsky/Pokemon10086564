Umbreon

Umbreon is a dark-type Pokémon that is known for its sleek and mysterious appearance. It is the evolved form of Eevee and is one of the most popular Pokémon in the franchise.

Catching Rate

The catching rate of Umbreon is determined by various factors, including the player's level, the type of Poké Ball used, and any status effects inflicted on the Pokémon. The base catching rate for wild Umbreon is typically around 45%.

Shiny Rate

Shiny Pokémon, including Shiny Umbreon, have a rare alternate coloration. The shiny rate for Umbreon, like most Pokémon, is approximately 1 in 4,096 encounters. This means it can be quite challenging to find a shiny Umbreon in the wild.
